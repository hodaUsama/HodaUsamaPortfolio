Short bio

I am a Senior Software Engineer with 17+ years of experience building scalable, high performance systems across insurance, e commerce, fintech, and enterprise platforms. My work spans architecture, backend engineering, API development, cloud readiness, and performance optimization for high transaction environments.

I spent 2 years at Microsoft supporting global clients on Microsoft Graph API, solving complex code level issues, optimizing integrations, and contributing to a 36% increase in customer satisfaction.

I combine deep .NET expertise with practical experience in distributed systems, automation, CI CD pipelines, and secure API design.

Alongside my industry career, I conduct academic research in AI and algorithm performance, focusing on optimization, machine learning models, and computer vision, with published work on MobileViT and algorithm design.

I also teach statistics, machine learning, and AI through my blog and YouTube channel, helping engineers and students understand advanced concepts in a simple way.
